How to handle 2 Cokes
Log them first thing in the morningAdd your 2 Cokes (280 cal) to MyFitnessPal at the start of the day so you know exactly what's left.
Pair with meals, not randomlyA Coke with lunch and one with dinner feels much more satisfying than drinking between meals.
Keep food tight on Coke daysYour food target becomes ~1,450–1,500 cal after accounting for the 2 Cokes.
Coke Zero for just one of them? 🎯Even swapping ONE saves 140 cal/day = ~1,000 cal/week. Totally optional — your call.
Sparkling water as backupTopo Chico or Bubly gives the carbonation hit without the sugar. Great between Cokes.
Bottom line: 2 Cokes a day is totally manageable. Stay on your protein, keep meals lean, and you will absolutely hit your August 24 goal.

The rules that actually matter
🥇 Protein first, alwaysEvery meal, ask yourself: where's my protein? This is the single most important habit.
🍽️ Don't skip mealsSkipping backfires — you get ravenous, overeat later, and lose muscle instead of fat.
💧 80–100 oz water dailyMore on lift days. Hydration directly improves performance and reduces cravings.
🛒 30 min Sunday prepBoil eggs, cook rice, portion snacks. Your whole week gets dramatically easier.
🎉 The 80/20 ruleOn plan 80% of the time. The other 20% — enjoy your life. Consistency beats perfection.
